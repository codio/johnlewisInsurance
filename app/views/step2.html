Consider how long your major financial responsibilities are likely to last e.g. paying off a mortgage, bringing up children. You can take out our Life Insurance for up to 30 years or until the age of 70.

Cover type

Level term cover is typically used to cover a debt that doesn’t reduce over time e.g. an interest only mortgage or to provide a fixed amount to a loved one.
Decreasing value cover is typically used to cover a debt that reduces over time e.g. a repayment mortgage. We have assumed a 6% per year mortgage interest rate in our policy.

Include Critical Illness

Critical illness provides cover if you suffer a critical illness such as a heart attack, stroke, cancer diagnosis (refer to the Policy Summary for a list of all illnesses we cover).
