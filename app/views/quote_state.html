Single or Joint policy… A joint life policy is typically cheaper than two single life policies. But while both cover two people’s lives, a joint life policy only pays out once, for whoever dies first, while with separate single life policies both will pay out if you both die.

Why do we still need to know your gender? While we no longer use your gender to determine the cost of your policy, some of our health questions are only relevant if you're male or female.
